Radioactive Decay

Modeling radioactive decay is important for understanding nuclear waste and its long-term impact.

There seems to be very little widespread, accurate knowledge of what "nuclear waste" means, how we deal with it, or how waste differs across reactor types. Check out the demo comparing waste profiles for different types of nuclear reactors.

Fission and Neutron Absorption

Modeling fission is important if you want to simulate reactor cores. Neither of these interactions is implemented yet because there isn't a powerful enough linear algebra library for in-browser numerical modeling, and the underlying physics is really complicated and sensitive to things like geometry... complicated! But I'd love to move this direction.
