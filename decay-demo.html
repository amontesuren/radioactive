Fission and Neutron Absorption

Modeling fission is important if you want to simulate reactor cores. These are not yet implemented. There are two main reasons: there doesn't yet seem to be a solid enough linear algebra library for in-browser numerical modeling, and the underlying physics is really complicated and sensitive to things like geometry... complicated! But I'd love to move this direction.
